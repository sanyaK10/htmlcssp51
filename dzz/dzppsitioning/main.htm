Famous Writers

Oscar Wilde

Irish poet and playwright. Famous for "The Picture of Dorian Gray" and brilliant wit.

William Wordsworth

English Romantic poet who helped to launch the Romantic Age in English literature.

Miguel de Cervantes

Spanish writer widely regarded as the greatest novelist, creator of "Don Quixote".

Charles Dickens

English writer and social critic. Created some of the world's best-known fictional characters.

Edgar Allan Poe

American writer, poet and critic, best known for his poetry and short stories of mystery.

Anne Hathaway

The wife of William Shakespeare, the famous English poet and playwright.
